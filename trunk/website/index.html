Welcome to Protique, providers of Enterprise Open Source Solutions for the modern enterprise.

We develop and support a range of Enterprise-scale open source middleware products as well as providing custom enhancements, consultancy and open source outsourcing services.

All of our products are available under an Apache 2.0 licence (BSD style) which allows you to easily embed them into your commerical or open source solutions. In particular we integrate all of our products cleanly into the following containers

  • Apache Geronimo
  • Spring
  • PicoContainer
